Agonda Beach

Agonda Beach is a public beach located in Agonda village in Goa, India, about 9.2 kilometres (5.7 mi) north of Palolem Beach in South Goa district, and about an hour from Margao. During the month of September, the beach serves as a nesting ground for olive ridley sea turtles.
